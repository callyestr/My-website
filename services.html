Service

Web Service

Platform for outside activities

Why?

I like to mingle with various people in various fields in school or outside. Many people apply for organization of administration and team leader. Most of the students, including me, get to know their organization's recruiting schedule through web pages, posters and social networking sites before they start applying. After that, when they begin some activities, they will communicate with each other through the organization's Kakao Talk.
I imagined how good it would be for organizations to be able to support applicants in more concise processes, and for management to integrate data and documents that make them efficiently. So I'd like to create a web/app service that allows organizations to recruit and operate members and integrate all the processes. This is an interactive service that can be used by both management and applicants of a particular organization. Applicants can see the organizations they are recruiting at a glance and see what their main activities are. They can also send a self-introduction statement and portfolio directly in this platform. If I add collaborative chat services like slack to this service, you'll be able to work more precisely.
At the same time, organizations can know applicants, compile related documents, and share simple documents through meetings within the platform. While working as a management team, you're often able to access relevant materials via e-mail, and if you can manage them with the web and apps, you can create more efficient processes and results. In addition, we can manage important accounting documents such as organization membership fees through the service, and if we open them to the members, we expect that both team members and management will be satisfied and each other will build trust. Finally, they will be able to introduce the main activities of the group to the volunteers, thereby simultaneously bringing about promotional effects.
